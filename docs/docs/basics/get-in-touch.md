# Getting in Touch

The TART community consists has two primary means of getting in touch.

## Bi-weekly video conference

There is a video-conference that is held every two weeks. The links for this are in pinned comments in the telegram chat.

## Telegram Chats

The TART [telegram channel](https://t.me/+u-2yzql__ggwYzAz#) is where most informal contact is made. If you're new to TART, join the chat and say hi.
