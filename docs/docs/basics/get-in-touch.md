# Getting in Touch

There are several ways to connect with the TART community.

## Project Calendar

Our [calendar of TART events](https://calendar.google.com/calendar/embed?src=c_5ce97b224a48240c1c5eb62275dea7c775bf4eca9a0c79593a7a59e1c485f5cc%40group.calendar.google.com) lists upcoming workshops and video conference sessions all in one place.

## Bi-weekly Video Conference

A community video conference is held every two weeks on Wednesday at 10am UTC+2. All are welcome. [Join on Zoom](https://www.google.com/url?q=https%3A%2F%2Fotago.zoom.us%2Fj%2F98553468309%3Fpwd%3DS0IyUzV5UU1IU1NML2hadnNNQ3ZEUT09&sa=D&source=calendar&ust=1782292320000000&usg=AOvVaw3tfLkIq16Rp4L7W5lhmpUb)

## Supervision Video Conference

On the alternate Wednesdays (the weeks without the community call), we hold a TART project supervision meeting where students working on TART-related projects meet and discuss their progress. All are welcome. [Join on Meeting](https://meet.google.com/kyy-quzu-mmx)

## Telegram Chat

The [TART Telegram channel](https://t.me/+u-2yzql__ggwYzAz#) is where most informal conversation happens. If you're new to TART and would like to see what is going on, join the chat and say hi!
