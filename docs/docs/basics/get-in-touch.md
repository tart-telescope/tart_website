# Getting in Touch

The TART community consists has two primary means of getting in touch.

## Project Calendar

We have a [calendar of TART events](https://calendar.google.com/calendar/embed?src=c_5ce97b224a48240c1c5eb62275dea7c775bf4eca9a0c79593a7a59e1c485f5cc%40group.calendar.google.com&ctz=Pacific%2FAuckland) which shows upcoming workshops as well as the video conference sessions.

## Bi-weekly video conference

There is a video-conference that is held every two weeks on Wednesday at 10am UTC+2: [Zoom Link](https://www.google.com/url?q=https%3A%2F%2Fotago.zoom.us%2Fj%2F98553468309%3Fpwd%3DS0IyUzV5UU1IU1NML2hadnNNQ3ZEUT09&sa=D&source=calendar&ust=1782292320000000&usg=AOvVaw3tfLkIq16Rp4L7W5lhmpUb)

## Supervision video conference

On the alternate Wednesdays there is a TART project supervision meeting where students working on TART-related projects meet and discuss progress. All are welcome  [Zoom Link](https://www.google.com/url?q=https%3A%2F%2Fotago.zoom.us%2Fj%2F98553468309%3Fpwd%3DS0IyUzV5UU1IU1NML2hadnNNQ3ZEUT09&sa=D&source=calendar&ust=1782292320000000&usg=AOvVaw3tfLkIq16Rp4L7W5lhmpUb)


## Telegram Chats

The TART [telegram channel](https://t.me/+u-2yzql__ggwYzAz#) is where most informal contact is made. If you're new to TART, join the chat and say hi.
