About Us

The TART is a 24 element synthesis array radio-telescope. It observes the entire sky continuously and is optimized to detect transient events, including satellites, near-earth objects and high-energy cosmic rays.

The telescope is also designed to serve as a platform for the development of new imaging algorithms.

The TART telescope is an open source project. Development is hosted on github.
